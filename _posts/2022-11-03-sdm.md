Today at work, I found that I am not careful to view the problem, always careless, for example, 63 environment start pcms service is alwasy stopped, with about 3 hours to see this problem, later found that the watch_pcms service is running to cause the problem, After fixing this problem later, it was not completely tested and sent to the implementation staff. In the afternoon, I was asked if I could get the alarms corresponding to the MCU, and I said it was difficult, because I was getting all the alarms, Then another one sent a picture showing the alarms under MCU, and I realized that I could get the alarms.
* Through these things, I found that I always do things carelessly. For some problems do not grasp all, only understand part, and do not continue to study and improve, which shows that I do not explore the spirit.
* For both technology, and work, you should be brave to explore and research, and pay more attention to some details, when doing things, it is better to analyze comprehensively and make records.

今天在工作中发现，我查看问题不细心，总是粗心，比如，63环境启动pcms服务总是被停止，用了大概3个小时看这问题，后面发现是watch_pcms服务正在运行导致的问题。后面修复这问题后，也没有完全的测试就发送给实施人员了。下午同时问我是否能获取到MCU对应的告警，我说这比较难，应为我当时是获取所有的告警。后面另一个同时发送了一张图片显示是MCU下的告警，我才发现是能获取到对应MCU的告警，我之前没有仔细的看接口。
* 通过这些事情，发现我做事总是粗心大意。对于一些问题没有掌握全部，只明白部分，也不继续研究和提升，这表明我没有探索精神。
* 对于技术和工作，都要勇于探索和研究，做事的时候要多注意一些细节。最好全面的分析并做记录。

感恩练习：
* Thanks to my colleagues for letting me know that I am careless again and have no spirit of exploration.
* 感谢同事让我知道我又粗心，没有探索精神。
* 感谢心理学书籍，让我知道上面的方法有效
* 感谢上帝能让我还活着，感谢生命又让我成长了一天
* 感谢书籍提升了我的认知
