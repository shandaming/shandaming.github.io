Yesterday I stayed up late palying games again, and today I'm also playing games. These 2 days I found that after playing games, I'm addicted to them, other people look for me, I dislike them to disturb me, and also when playing games, my concentration is very hight, my blood pressure and eye pressure are rising, and I also have a headache, after two days, I alos found that my eye prescription is rising again, and yesterday I did not practice English and writing jourmal, I always feel like playing games. I don't have enough time to play games. To sum up, playing games is more harmful than reading novels.

昨天又熬夜玩游戏了，今天也是玩游戏，这2天我发现，玩游戏后，我向上瘾了一样，其他人找我，我很嫌弃他们打扰我，还有玩游戏时注意力很集中，我的血压和眼压都在升高，而且我还头胀痛，两天下来后，我也发现我的眼睛度数又升高了，而且昨天没有练习英语和写日志。总结下来，玩游戏的危害比看小说还大。
