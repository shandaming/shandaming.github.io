Today in the busy VDC interface debugging. I encountered with VDC account login interface call after reporting errors, to the group feedback, after the letter of the week pointed out that the use of SMC account to login to VDC the test is successful. I found that I did not test carefully, encountered a little problem did not sort out again under, still need to strengthen the ability to explore.

今天在忙VDC接口调试，我遇到用VDC账号登陆接口调用报错后，就向群里反馈，后面周信指出用SMC账号来登陆VDC，测试是成功的。我发现我没有仔细的测试，遇到一点问题就没有再次梳理下，还需要加强探索能力。

Thinking about the problem of blind dates, before I met the other party ignored me, I thought she was unwilling, and then deleted her, thinking that the back can be found, but the back can be found is an unknown time, there may not find the right one, And from delete to in find, which may take six months to a year. I'm too old to waste now. So before venting emotions, impulsive, reckless messages scolded them, delete them, I am the most stupid wrong behavior, is irrational, unforgivable. Time no longer allows me to be so impulsive and reckless. Must be determined to avoid deleting. To think rationally, think about the regretful feelings and lessons learned before.

想了下相亲对象的问题，之前我遇到对方不理我，我就认为她不愿意，然后就删除了她，认为后面能找到，但是后面能找到是个未知时间，有可能找不到合适的。而从删除到在找到，这可能要半年到一年的时间。我现在年龄很大了，已经不能浪费了。所以之前发泄情绪，冲动，莽撞的发信息骂她们，删除她们，我这是愚蠢至极的错误行为，是不理性的、不可饶恕的。时间已经不允许我在这么冲动莽撞了。必须要坚决的避免删除。要理性的思考，想想之前的后悔心情和教训。
