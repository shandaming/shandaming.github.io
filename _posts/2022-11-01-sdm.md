Today the test raised alarms without history information. I found that with history information, it also allows me to locate the problem well, so I added it. I didn't read the email sent by Mr Luo yesterday, which led me to not understand why Zhou Wei asked me for the procedure, or Mr Luo reminded me, I didn't make a habit of checking my emails every day. When Mr Luo discussed the problem with me. I was actually impatient, but communicating across the phone, I found that I had small mood swings.
* In general, I did not consider the product requirements clearly, and some requirements were not considered. I was looking at the problem from the developer's point of view, not from the user's point of view, and I still had impatience, and my emotions were affected by the site evironment and other people's emotions, which needed to be overcome.
* To consider the requirement function carefully, both from the development point of view and from the user's point of view. To overcome my impatience, to think rationally, and to avoid impulsiveness and recklessness.

今天测试提出告警没有历史信息，我发现有历史信息后，也能让我好定位问题，我就加上了。罗总昨天发的邮件，我没有看，导致我不理解周徽为什么向我要程序，还是罗总提醒了我。我没有养成每天查看邮件的习惯。罗宗与我讨论问题的时候，其实我是很不耐烦，但是隔着电话沟通，我发现我情绪波动小。
* 总的来说，对于产品需求没有考虑清楚，有些需求也没有考虑到，我是站在开发的角度看问题，而不是用户的角度看问题，我还是有不耐烦的情绪，情绪也会受到现场环境和他人的情绪影响，这需要克服。
* 要仔细的考虑需求功能，既要站在开发的角度考虑也要站在用户的角度考虑。要克服自己的不耐烦情绪，要理性思考，禁忌冲动和莽撞。
