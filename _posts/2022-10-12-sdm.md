When i was writing the code today, Pan Long came to ask me the reason for the environmental error. Let me see. I was busy writing the code at the time, but i didn't want to help him read it. I was impatient and impatience. I read it for him later and found that there was a problem with the interface he sent. I replied to him in a loud and excited tone, and my speech was a bit rushed. Later, Peng Huiquan came to show me the problem of SMC3.0 failure in the product environment. I was also impatient and impatience.
* I'm impatient, impatience, talking to people, not controlling my emotions, and not thinking rationally. I'm really rubbish.
* You must be patient, not impatient, speak calmly. Control your emotions, think rationally, and think in empathy.

今天我在写代码时，潘龙来问我环境报错的原因，让我看看，我当时忙着写代码，心理不想帮他看，不耐烦，急躁，后面帮他看过了，发现是他发送的接口有问题，我当时就用激动的大声的语气回复他，说话有些冲人。后面彭灰泉来让我看产品环境SMC3.0故障的问题，我也是不耐烦，急躁。
* 我又不耐烦，急躁，说话冲人，没有控制好情绪，也没有理性思考，我真是垃圾阿。
* 必须要耐烦，不急躁，说话平和，控制情绪，理性思考，要换位思考。
